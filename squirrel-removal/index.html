Unusual Noises

One of the most common signs of squirrels in your home is the presence of strange noises coming from areas like your attic, walls, or chimney. these sounds might include scratching, gnawing, scurrying, or even chattering. Squirrels are most active during the daytime, especially in the early morning and late afternoon. If you hear these noises regularly during daylight hours, particularly in your attic or wall voids, it's a strong indicator of a squirrel presence. The noises may become more pronounced as the squirrel population grows. Their constant movement and activity within confined spaces can be quite loud and disruptive to the quiet of your home, significantly impacting your peace and quiet. Pay close attention to the timing and location of these sounds; they can provide valuable clues about the extent and location of the infestation. Squirrels are skilled climbers and opportunistic creatures, capable of finding or creating openings to access your property.
